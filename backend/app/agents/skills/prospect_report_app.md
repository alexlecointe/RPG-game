# Skill: Rapport prospection — App mobile

Applique les instructions generiques de prospect_report, plus ces specificites app mobile :

## Analyse supplementaire

### Prospects app
- Tech bloggers (articles review, comparatifs, tops apps)
- YouTubers review (chaines tech, unboxing apps, tutoriels)
- Podcasts tech (interviews, segments apps de la semaine)
- Communautes Reddit/Product Hunt (early adopters, power users)

### Criteres app
- Audience technique vs grand public (alignement avec le produit)
- Plateforme iOS/Android (couverture et preference)
- Historique reviews (qualite, frequence, ton)

### Canaux app
- Product Hunt launch (preparation, timing, communaute)
- Beta communities (TestFlight, betalist, BetaBound)
- Press kit envoi (media kit, screenshots HD, demo video)
- App review sites (AppAdvice, 148Apps, sites specialises)

### Launch strategy
- Pre-launch waitlist (landing page, collecte emails)
- Beta testers (recrutement, feedback loop, iterations)
- Early adopters (programme ambassadeur, acces exclusif)
- Press embargo (coordination timing, exclusivite media)
