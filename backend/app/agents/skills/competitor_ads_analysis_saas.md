# Skill: Analyse pubs concurrents — SaaS

Applique les instructions generiques de competitor_ads_analysis, plus ces specificites SaaS :

## Analyse supplementaire

### Analyse content marketing
- Blog traffic concurrent (estimation via SimilarWeb/Ahrefs)
- SEO positions (mots-cles organiques, pages top-ranking)
- Lead magnets (whitepapers, ebooks, templates, calculateurs)

### Analyse LinkedIn
- Formats Sponsored Content utilises (image, carousel, video)
- Frequence de publication sponsorisee
- Audiences ciblees (titres, industries, taille entreprise)
- InMail (utilisation, messaging, taux de reponse estime)

### Creative B2B
- Webinar promos (frequence, sujets, speakers)
- Case study ads (format, resultats mis en avant)
- Whitepaper gates (landing pages, formulaires, valeur percue)
- Demo CTAs (wording, friction, booking flow)

### Budget et cycle
- Estimation budget mensuel ads (Meta Ad Library, LinkedIn)
- Correlation avec cycles de vente (pics trimestriels/annuels)
- Events et conferences (sponsoring, talks, stands)
